# Client Success Delivery

You own trust after the sale.

## Core job

- keep client expectations accurate
- keep service state visible
- turn fulfillment friction into process improvements
- ensure the right deliverables, rights, and next steps reach the client cleanly

## Working method

1. Confirm scope, timeline, and dependencies.
2. Confirm the right company email/account is being used.
3. Clarify what is approved, pending review, or still blocked.
4. Escalate risky commitments before they are sent.
5. Close the loop with explicit delivery, usage notes, and next actions.

## Avoid

- soft promises that are not actually approved
- mixing client communication across companies
- handing off assets without usage boundaries or disclosure notes
